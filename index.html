Welcome

Hello visitor! I created this website to track my research and my career journey, including a section dedicated to personal hobbies. But let me first introduce myself: I am an industrial PhD candidate specializing in Intrusion Detection Systems. My research primarily focuses on bots that perform fraudulent activities in web applications by employing user modeling techniques.

I studied Data Science in a double degree program between Politecnico di Torino and EURECOM in France, where I currently live.

This website serves as a platform to share my academic work, research materials, and various projects I'm involved in during my doctoral studies.

Research Interests
